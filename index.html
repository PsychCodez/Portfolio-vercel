// About Me

Hi, I'm Dhanush Reddy — a final-year Computer Science and Engineering student with a deep-rooted curiosity for how systems work under the hood. I'm not someone who’s confined to a single lane — I enjoy stepping outside my comfort zone and exploring new domains, which is why my project list is diverse. But it’s not a case of “jack of all trades, master of none” — I’m driven by a genuine desire to understand the why and how behind everything I build.

What captivates me most isn’t just the final product, but the work behind the beauty — the invisible logic, infrastructure, and intelligence that powers great technology. Whether it’s how a machine makes a decision, how a containerized system handles failure, or how data flows through a real-time pipeline — I’m always peeling back the layers to learn more.

While I’ve dabbled across multiple areas, I find myself increasingly drawn toward AI and Machine Learning. I see immense potential there — both in terms of impact and innovation — and I'm working toward carving out something meaningful in that space.

Some things I value deeply:

  • Curiosity over complacency — I'm always asking questions and diving deep
  • Learning by building — I prefer hands-on exploration to passive consumption
  • Systems thinking — I like connecting the dots across disciplines
  • Clarity in complexity — I enjoy simplifying hard problems through logic and structure

If there’s one thing that defines me, it’s this: I love knowing how things work — not just what they do.
